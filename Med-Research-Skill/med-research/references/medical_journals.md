# Top-Tier Medical & Radiology Journals (JCR Q1/Q2)

For all Medical Deep Research tasks, prioritize the following journals and their associated Impact Factors (2024 approximate).

## 1. General Medical (The "Big Five")
- **New England Journal of Medicine (NEJM)** (IF: 158.5) - Highest impact.
- **The Lancet** (IF: 145.0)
- **Journal of the American Medical Association (JAMA)** (IF: 120.7)
- **The BMJ** (IF: 105.7)
- **Nature Medicine** (IF: 82.9)

## 2. Radiology & Medical Imaging (Core for Lead Apron/FHR)
- **Radiology** (IF: 19.7, Q1) - RSNA official journal.
- **Journal of the American College of Radiology (JACR)** (IF: 6.2, Q1)
- **Medical Physics** (IF: 3.8, Q2) - AAPM official journal.
- **European Radiology** (IF: 5.9, Q1)
- **American Journal of Roentgenology (AJR)** (IF: 5.0, Q1)
- **IEEE Transactions on Medical Imaging** (IF: 10.6, Q1)
- **Nature Communications** (IF: 14.7, Q1) - Strong for MedSAM/AI.

## 3. Digital Health & AI in Medicine
- **Nature npj Digital Medicine** (IF: 15.2, Q1)
- **The Lancet Digital Health** (IF: 30.8, Q1)
- **Frontiers in Digital Health** (IF: 3.2, Q2)
- **Health Information Science and Systems** (IF: 3.4, Q2)

## 4. Radiation Protection & Health Physics
- **Health Physics** (IF: 1.2, Q2/Q3) - IRPA official.
- **Radiation Protection Dosimetry** (IF: 1.0, Q3)
- **Journal of Radiological Protection** (IF: 1.4, Q3)
