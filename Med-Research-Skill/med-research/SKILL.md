# Medical Research Skill v7.8 (Final Master)

## Core Capabilities
1. **Multi-Source Mining**: PubMed + ClinicalTrials + ArXiv + IEEE integration.
2. **Consensus Engine**: Claim-based synthesis and evidence grading.
3. **Smart Traceability (v7.8 Master)**: 
    - **Dual-Link Protocol**: Mandatory `[Local](<../papers/filename.pdf>) | [Web](URL)` format.
    - **Relative Portability**: All local links use `../papers/` and bracketed pathing for spaces.

## Reporting Standard (v7.8 Master Matrix)
- **High-Density 10-Column Matrix**:
    1. Title (Full) | 2. Relevance | 3. Core Architecture | 4. Contribution/Data | 5. Technical Insight | 6. JCR/IF (Year) | 7. Data Type/Eng | 8. Bottleneck | 9. Future Utility | 10. REF
- **Structural Deep Dives (Post-Matrix)**:
    - **Mathematical/Algorithmic Modeling**: Analysis of complexity ($O(N)$, $O(N^2)$), layer-wise logic.
    - **Data Engineering Bottleneck**: Preprocessing, labeling bias, sensor fusion analysis.
    - **Clinical Mapping**: Actionable project alignment to hospital datasets.

## Standard Research Loop (v7.8 Protocol)
1. **Scope**: Define target papers (Dynamic row sizing based on relevance).
2. **Retrieve**: Dual-link identification (Local + Online).
3. **Synthesize**: Generate High-Density 10-Column Matrix.
4. **Deep Dive**: Perform full technical breakdown.
5. **Finalize**: Commit to repository.
