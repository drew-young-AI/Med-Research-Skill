# Detailed Summary: PRPE_QC_No_Xray (2025)

## Methodology
- **Concept**: Predictive QC without radiation exposure, using physical/usage metadata.
- **Features**: Equipment age, usage frequency, folding/storage habits, visible exterior wear, and weight changes (detecting lead shift).
- **Algorithms**: Gradient Boosting (XGBoost) and Random Forest classifiers.

## Results
- **Performance**: High sensitivity in identifying high-risk equipment before formal X-ray inspection.
- **Clinical Value**: Acts as a pre-screening tool to reduce labor and radiation costs in hospital PPE management.
