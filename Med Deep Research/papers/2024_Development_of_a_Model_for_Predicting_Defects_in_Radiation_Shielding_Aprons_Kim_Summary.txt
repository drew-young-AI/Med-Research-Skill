# Detailed Summary: Kim et al. (2024) - Lead Apron Defects

## Methodology
- **Core**: CNN-MATLAB based prediction model for microcracks.
- **Specimens**: 0.125mm and 0.25mm lead equivalent (SKMD lightweight).
- **Validation**: Compared automated AI recognition against human visual/fluoroscopic inspection.

## Results
- **0.25 mmPb accuracy**: 87.7% (AI) vs 56.0% (Human).
- **0.125 mmPb accuracy**: 94.49% (AI) vs 51.5% (Human).
- **Conclusion**: AI significantly outperforms human experts in identifying early-stage micro-cracks, especially in thinner protective fabrics.
