# Detailed Summary: CTGFusionNet (2025)

## Methodology
- **Multi-modal Fusion**: Combines FHR and UC signals using a dual-branch deep learning framework.
- **Architecture**: Bi-CNN for local morphology + LSTM/Transformer for long-term temporal dependencies.
- **Attention Mechanism**: Dynamically weights FHR vs. UC features to identify critical signs of fetal distress.
- **Preprocessing**: Denoising, interpolation, and standardization of clinical CTG signals.

## Results
- **Performance**: Significant improvement in 3-class classification (Normal, Suspicious, Pathological) over baseline ML models (SVM, Random Forest).
- **Impact**: Reduced false-positive rates in clinical decision-making for emergency C-sections.
